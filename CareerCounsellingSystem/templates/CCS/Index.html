Introduction

In an era of ever-evolving knowledge and cutting-edge technologies, it has become essential to narrow the gap between academia and industry. The role of career counseling and job placement centers has therefore assumed an important role as they extend various trainings and student services ranging from resume writing and presentational skills to interviewing techniques and placements.

The main aim of career counselling is to help students choose a field that is in tune with their skills and their job expectations. Thus, with the help of career counselling, most candidates end up choosing the right career, and perform their level best, which ultimately helps them succeed.
